The goal on Centum is to fill the 100 squares on the board. 

Starting by selecting one and jumping 1 diagonally or 2 horizontally/vertically.

THe game ends when you fill the 100 squares or if you run out of moves!

Try it!

Enjoy the Centum Hit made by Ricardo Martín Castillo.

Master Mode

Try to reach the goal to grow and earn points.
